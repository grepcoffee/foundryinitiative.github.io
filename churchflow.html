Features

Everything your team
needs on Sunday morning

ChurchFlow was built from real church needs — not product roadmaps. Every feature exists because a real congregation needed it.

✅
Live Check-In Tracking
Check people in with a single tap. As people arrive, they drop off the list automatically — keeping your view clean and focused on who's still outstanding.
🔄
Planning Center Sync
One-tap sync pulls your full congregation from Planning Center Online — including household groupings, membership types, and inactive member removal.
📧
Automated Absence Reports
After service, send a formatted absence report to your pastoral team with one click — including attendance percentage, absent member details, and contact info.
👋
Visitor Management
Log first-time visitors and automatically create them in Planning Center as a Visitor membership type — keeping your database clean and follow-up easy.
👶
Kids Check-In
Separate kids check-in flow with matching codes for secure pickup. Supports multiple service types and custom age group configuration.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Five distinct roles — Super Admin, Admin, Manager, Check-In, and Viewer — so every team member has exactly the access they need, nothing more.
🌙
Dark Mode
Automatic dark mode based on system preference, with a manual toggle. Designed to look great on any device — tablet, phone, or desktop at the welcome table.
📊
Exclusion Management
Exclude specific individuals from absence reports — snowbirds, long-term travelers, or anyone your team knows is out — without permanently removing them.
📋
Service History
Every service is logged. Go back and see who was present on any given Sunday, pull historical reports, and track attendance trends over time.
How It Works

Simple Sunday morning workflow

ChurchFlow is built around a clear three-step process that your whole team can follow without training.

01 — SYNC
Pull from Planning Center
Before service, tap Sync to pull the latest member data from Planning Center — new people, household updates, and inactive member removals all handled automatically.
02 — CHECK IN
Mark People as They Arrive
As members arrive, check them in. The list updates in real-time — checked-in people clear from the view so you always see who's still coming. Add walk-in visitors on the spot.
03 — REPORT
Send the Absence Report
After service, preview the absence list and send it to your pastoral team with one click. The email includes attendance stats, absent member details, and contact information.
Under the Hood

Built to run anywhere,
owned by your church.

ChurchFlow is self-hosted — your data stays on your hardware. No SaaS subscriptions, no data sharing, no per-seat pricing.

🖥️ Infrastructure

  • Runs on any Node.js server — Mac mini, Raspberry Pi, VPS, or cloud VM
  • SQLite database with WAL mode for reliable concurrent access
  • Cloudflare Tunnel support for secure public HTTPS access
  • LaunchDaemon configuration for auto-start on macOS

🔗 Integrations

  • Planning Center People API — full member sync with household support
  • Planning Center Services API — service type and plan management
  • Resend email API for reliable absence report delivery
  • Better Stack (Logtail) for structured log monitoring

🔒 Security

  • bcrypt password hashing with configurable rounds
  • AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive stored credentials
  • Session-based auth with account lockout after failed attempts
  • Rate limiting on login and visitor submission endpoints

⚙️ Built With

  • Node.js + Express — fast, lightweight server
  • better-sqlite3 — synchronous SQLite with zero native dependencies
  • Vanilla JavaScript frontend — no framework, no build step
  • CSS custom properties for full dark/light mode theming
