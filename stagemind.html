Features

One system to
orchestrate everything

StageMind was built from the real frustration of managing a show across five different apps, three operators, and zero coordination.

📋
Unified Rundown
One master document controls every system. Lighting cues, slide transitions, stream scene changes, audio triggers, and video playback all live in a single ordered rundown your whole team works from.
🔌
OSC & MIDI Control
Send OSC messages to lighting desks, QLab, Resolume, and any other hardware that speaks the protocol. Trigger MIDI cues for instrument and effects racks. Full two-way communication — StageMind listens as well as commands.
✨
AI Stage Manager
Before the show, the AI reviews your rundown and connected systems — flagging timing conflicts, missing cues, offline connections, and common failure patterns. It learns from past shows to get smarter over time.
🎬
ProPresenter Integration
Deep two-way integration with ProPresenter 7 — advance slides, jump to specific items, trigger looks, and read back the current slide state. Works over the ProPresenter network API on your local network.
📹
Streaming Control
Switch OBS scenes, trigger vMix transitions, and control Blackmagic ATEM inputs directly from your rundown. Your stream operator stays in sync without needing a separate script.
▶️
Live Mode
A distraction-free full-screen view built for the moment the show is running. Large GO button, clear current/next cue display, real-time system status, and instant access to manual overrides if something goes sideways.
👥
Multi-Operator Support
Multiple team members can connect simultaneously — the TD fires cues, the slides operator follows along, the stream team sees what's coming. Everyone stays in sync without a walkie-talkie.
✅
Pre-Show Checklist
Automated pre-flight checks verify every connected system before doors open. StageMind tests OSC paths, pings integrations, and confirms hardware is responding — so you find out about problems before the audience does.
📊
Show History & Notes
Every show is logged — cue timings, overrides, connection issues, and operator notes. Review what happened after any event, spot recurring problems, and build a living record of how your production runs.
How It Works

From build to broadcast

StageMind is organized around a clear three-phase workflow — so your team is prepared before the show starts, not scrambling after it does.

01 — BUILD
Design Your Rundown
Add every cue to the unified rundown — lighting looks, slide cues, stream transitions, audio triggers, video playback. Arrange, group, and annotate. When the rundown is right, every operator is reading from the same page.
02 — CHECK
Let the AI Review It
Before going live, the AI Stage Manager analyzes your rundown and all connected systems. It surfaces conflicts, missing cues, and offline hardware — giving your team time to fix issues before they become problems in front of an audience.
03 — RUN
Fire the Show
Switch to Live Mode and run the show from a single interface. Hit GO to fire each cue — StageMind sends the commands to every connected system simultaneously. Your operators watch it happen; they don't scramble to make it happen.
Under the Hood

Built for real production
environments.

StageMind runs on your own hardware — on your local network, close to your gear. No cloud dependency, no latency, no single point of failure outside your control.

🖥️ Infrastructure

  • Runs on any Node.js server on your local production network
  • WebSocket server for real-time multi-operator sync across devices
  • SQLite for rundown storage, show history, and configuration
  • Designed for dedicated Mac mini or small-form-factor PC in the booth

🔗 Protocols & Integrations

  • OSC (Open Sound Control) — lighting desks, QLab, Resolume, and custom hardware
  • MIDI — USB and network MIDI for instrument and effects control
  • ProPresenter 7 Network API — bi-directional slide control
  • OBS WebSocket, vMix API, and Blackmagic ATEM SDK for stream control

✨ AI Capabilities

  • Pre-show rundown analysis — conflict detection and missing cue identification
  • Connection health monitoring with actionable remediation suggestions
  • Cue timing recommendations based on historical show data
  • Pluggable AI backend — works with Claude API, OpenAI, or local models

⚙️ Built With

  • Node.js + Express — lightweight server optimized for low-latency cue firing
  • Socket.IO — real-time multi-client sync for multi-operator workflows
  • better-sqlite3 — fast synchronous storage with no native dependencies
  • Vanilla JavaScript frontend — no framework, no build step, runs on any device in the booth
